## Insights
#### Positive Growth Trend:
1.Forecasting shows steady sales growth in coming years, particularly in fresh produce.
2. Store Performance: Large city outlets (Tier 3) generate more sales compared to smaller city outlets (Tier 1).
#### Forecasting Trends
1. Predictive analysis indicates a steady growth in sales for upcoming years.
2. Growth is particularly strong in fresh food categories.
#### Correlation Analysis
1. Item Weight vs. Sales: Positive correlation - heavier items tend to drive higher sales.
2. Item Visibility vs. Sales: Very weak negative correlation (r = -0.00131) product visibility does not significantly affect sales.
## Recommendations
- Invest in High-Revenue Categories like Fruits & Vegetables, Household, and Starchy Foods to boost sales.
- Improve Low-Revenue Categories such as Seafood with better pricing, promotions, and campaigns.
- Use Forecasting for Inventory Planning to avoid stock-outs and oversupply.
- Geographic Strategy: Focus expansion in Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities while tailoring promotions for Tier 3.
- Marketing Optimization: Redirect resources from visibility campaigns to pricing strategies and quality improvements.
